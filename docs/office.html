      Popular Questions

      • How much does cleaning cost?

        For houses and apartments we have a promotional monthly subscription for 1000$.

        The subscription includes 1 deep cleaning and 1 maintenance cleaning.

        Office and restaurant cleaning — 2zł per m².

        Post-renovation cleaning — 5zł per m².

      • What cleaning agents do we use?

        In our work we use only quality German equipment KARCHER and cleaning agents KIEHL. All our chemistry is absolutely safe and does not cause allergies.

      • How many hours does cleaning take?

        The duration of cleaning depends on the size of the premises and the degree of contamination. Our cleaners usually finish cleaning within 2-3 hours.

      • What is a cleaning subscription?

        Subscription - a unique promotional offer for a monthly cleaning package! The advantage of the subscription is that you get 1 deep cleaning and 1 technical cleaning per month at an affordable price. You don't need to spend time on re-ordering a cleaner, after subscribing we will come at the agreed time.

      • What guarantee of quality cleaning?

        We carefully monitor the quality of cleaning and collect feedback from each client. If you don't like the quality of cleaning, we will clean again in the coming days.

      • How to order and pay for cleaning?

        You can order and pay for cleaning directly on our website! Enter your details, date and address in the form and choose a convenient payment method (online or cash on delivery). Also, on our website you can calculate the cost of cleaning premises using the calculator!
