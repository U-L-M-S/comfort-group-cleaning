Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much does cleaning cost?

    For houses and apartments we offer a promotional monthly subscription for $1000.

    The subscription includes 1 deep cleaning and 1 maintenance cleaning.

    Office and restaurant cleaning — 2zł per m².

    After renovation cleaning — 5zł per m².

  • What cleaning products do we use?

    In our work we use only high-quality German equipment KARCHER and cleaning products KIEHL. All our chemicals are completely safe and do not cause allergies.

  • How many hours does cleaning take?

    The duration of cleaning depends on the size of the premises and the degree of contamination. Our cleaners usually finish cleaning within 2-3 hours.

  • What is a cleaning subscription?

    The subscription is a unique promotional offer for a monthly cleaning package! The advantage of the subscription is that you get 1 deep cleaning and 1 maintenance cleaning per month at an affordable price. You don't need to spend time on re-ordering a cleaner, after purchasing the subscription we will come at the agreed time.

  • What guarantee is there for quality cleaning?

    We carefully monitor the quality of cleaning and collect feedback from each client. If you don't like the quality of cleaning, we will clean again in the coming days.

  • How to order and pay for cleaning?

    You can order and pay for cleaning directly on our website! Enter your details, date and address in the form and choose a convenient payment method (online or cash on arrival). Also, on our website you can calculate the cost of cleaning premises using our href="./calc-order.html">calculator!

If you didn't find an answer to your question - ask it to our manager. We will contact you today and provide consultation.
